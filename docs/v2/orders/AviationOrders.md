# Aviation & Carrier Orders

These orders manage the flight deck operations of carriers, airbases, and helicopter-capable ships.

- **[READY_AIRCRAFT](./handlers/ReadyAircraft.md)**: Moves an aircraft from the hangar to the flight deck.
- **[LAUNCH_AIRCRAFT](./handlers/LaunchAircraft.md)**: Launches a readied aircraft into the simulation.
- **[RECOVER_AIRCRAFT](./handlers/RecoverAircraft.md)**: Commands an airborne aircraft to land.
