# Mission & Doctrine Orders

These orders configure high-level automated behaviors and organizational rules.

- **[ASSIGN_PATROL](./handlers/AssignPatrol.md)**: Assigns a unit to a Combat Air Patrol (CAP) or general patrol mission.
- **[SET_ROE_POSTURE](./handlers/SetRoePosture.md)**: Updates the Rules of Engagement (ROE) for specific domains.
- **[SET_WITHDRAW_CONDITION](./handlers/SetWithdrawCondition.md)**: Sets the conditions (Bingo/Winchester) for automatic RTB.
- **[INVESTIGATE_CONTACT](./handlers/InvestigateContact.md)**: Commands a unit to move toward a contact for identification.
- **[SET_EVASION_DOCTRINE](./handlers/SetEvasionDoctrine.md)**: Configures tactical responses to incoming threats.
