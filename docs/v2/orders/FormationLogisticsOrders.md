# Formation & Logistics Orders

These orders manage group organizational behavior and resource replenishment.

- **[JOIN_FORMATION](./handlers/JoinFormation.md)**: Assigns a unit to a station relative to a guide.
- **[LEAVE_FORMATION](./handlers/LeaveFormation.md)**: Removes a unit from its current formation.
- **[SET_FORMATION_SPRINT_DRIFT](./handlers/SetFormationSprintDrift.md)**: Applies Sprint and Drift policy to a group.
- **[REQUEST_REFUELING](./handlers/RequestRefueling.md)**: Commands a unit to seek out a tanker.
- **[INITIATE_UNREP](./handlers/InitiateUnrep.md)**: Initiates replenishment at sea.
- **[SET_DC_PRIORITY](./handlers/SetDcPriority.md)**: Sets the priority for Damage Control teams.
- **[SCUTTLE](./handlers/Scuttle.md)**: Commands the unit to self-destruct.
